## ðŸ’¡ **PRESENTATION TIPS**

### **Pacing**
- Allow 1-2 minutes per slide for the pitch deck (20-30 minutes total)
- Allow 10-15 minutes for the application walkthrough
- Reserve 10 minutes for Q&A

### **Tone**
- Confident but not arrogant
- Data-driven and specific (use exact numbers)
- Passionate about the mission
- Professional and investor-ready

### **Body Language**
- Maintain eye contact with all investors
- Use hand gestures to emphasize key points
- Stand/sit with open posture
- Smile when discussing social impact

### **Handling Numbers**
- Say "point" for decimals: "12.3" = "twelve point three"
- Say "trillion" or "billion" clearly and slowly
- Pause after big numbers to let them sink in
- Round when appropriate: "85%+" instead of "85.3%"
